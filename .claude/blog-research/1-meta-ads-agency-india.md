## Real data (with sources)
- Entry packages start ₹12,000 + 18% GST/mo. Typical ₹10,000–₹1,00,000+/mo (fee only, spend separate). [globaladvertisingmedia.com](https://globaladvertisingmedia.com/packages-of-facebook-ads.html), [upgrowth.in](https://upgrowth.in/facebook-advertising-pricing/)
- Serious-brand threshold: ₹2L+/mo retainer with ₹8L+/mo ad spend. [upgrowth.in](https://upgrowth.in/red-flags-meta-ads-agency-contracts/)
- Freelancer ₹25k–₹1.5L/mo; In-house 3-person ₹5L–₹9L/mo; Agency ₹40L–₹1.2Cr/yr. An agency at ₹2L/mo can have lower TCO than a ₹60k freelancer once overheads counted. [upgrowth.in](https://upgrowth.in/smm-agency-vs-freelancer-vs-inhouse/)
- Fee models: % of ad spend 10–20% (decreases as budget rises); below ~$8–12k/mo spend flat retainer is better value. 2026 survey mix: 42% flat / 31% % of spend / 27% hybrid. [lineardesign.com](https://lineardesign.com/blog/facebook-ads-management-pricing-guide/), [stackmatix.com](https://www.stackmatix.com/blog/advertising-agency-pricing-models-compared)
- Min ad spend to work: ₹30,000–₹50,000/mo. [techeasify.com](https://techeasify.com/minimum-budget-for-facebook-ads-in-india/)
- Avg agency-client relationship 3.2 yrs; first 90 days peak churn. [focus-digital.co](https://focus-digital.co/average-marketing-agency-churn/)
- Good Meta ROAS 2025 = 2x–4x. [inbeat.co](https://www.inbeat.co/articles/good-roas-for-facebook-ads/)

## Red flags
Guaranteed ROAS/overnight results; agency runs ads from THEIR ad account (you lose pixel/audience history on exit); refuses view-only Ads Manager access; vanity-metric reporting; bait-and-switch staffing; no creative testing system; 12-month lock-in with no performance escape clause; Business Manager owned by agency; punitive exit terms. upGrowth claims ~8 of 10 agency contracts contain a disqualifying red flag. [upgrowth.in](https://upgrowth.in/red-flags-meta-ads-agency-contracts/), [thebrandamp.com](https://www.thebrandamp.com/blog/why-businesses-must-own-their-ad-accounts-first-party-data-switching-agencies/)

## India-specific (strong differentiator)
- Agency fees attract 18% GST (advertising SAC). [busy.in](https://busy.in/gst-rates/digital-marketing-services/)
- Add your GSTIN to Meta ad account → billed B2B by Facebook India, no IGST/RCM. [rmpsco.com](https://rmpsco.com/understanding-gst-on-google-and-facebook-advertisements-in-india/)
- RCM applies if buying from overseas agency/freelancer (self-pay 18% IGST, claim ITC).
- 2025 update: 6% Equalisation Levy on online ads REMOVED from 1 April 2025 (Finance Act 2025). Fresh citable fact.
- ITC claimable on agency GST if registered — effectively recovers the 18%.
- Ownership norm: client owns Page, pixel, catalog, ad account; agency gets PARTNER access. [Meta Business Help](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/375950529870841)
- Scams: black-market Business Manager accounts, BM phishing/invite scams, Meta Partner Directory taken down (can't self-verify badge — demand documentation; badge ≠ quality guarantee).
